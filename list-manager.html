Universal Red Flags — Check Every Deal
🌊
Flood Zone / Wetlands
Check every deal. Kills value fast especially in Okeechobee, Charlotte, Lee counties.
🦅
Scrubjay Boundary
Critical for Charlotte, Sarasota, Lee. Check permit boundary before ANY offer.
🐢
Tortoises / Owls
Tortoises 6–7k cost, Owls up to 2–3k max 7k. Especially Lehigh, Marion, PSL.
⚡
Power Access
Primary value driver in Marion, Polk, Walton markets. No power = much lower ARV.
🛣️
Road Type
Paved vs dirt affects price significantly — especially Citrus Hills, Weeki Wachee, Beverly Hills.
💧
Dry vs Wet Lot
Wet lots severely discounted. Always verify — critical in South Gulf Cove, Cape Coral.
💰
Special Assessments
Check assessment balance. Can kill margins especially in Cape Coral N2 scenarios.
🏗️
Zoning / Future Zoning
Verify current and future zoning. Check w_srv_avail and s_srv_avail in Cape Coral.
